🧘 Quick Wellness Tips
🌬️
Box Breathing: Inhale 4s → Hold 4s → Exhale 4s → Hold 4s. Repeat 4 times to calm your nervous system.
👁️
20-20-20 Rule: Every 20 mins, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds to reduce eye strain.
💧
Stay Hydrated: Drink a glass of water right now. Dehydration increases cortisol (stress hormone).
🚶
Micro Walk: Stand up and take a 2-minute walk. Even short movement boosts mood and focus significantly.
